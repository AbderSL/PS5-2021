DualSense: a brand new controller

The PlayStation will come with a new controller, and even a brand new controller. Sony has indeed created the surprise by unveiling it  in April 2020, since it completely breaks with the DualShock series

It is therefore not a DualShock 5 that must be waited for, but a DualSense, in reference in particular to the new haptic feedback technology designed to offer a "rich panel of sensations, like a slight feeling of heaviness while driving a vehicle in the mud ” . It will replace the “vibration” technology used in the DualShock series, and will be accompanied by “adaptive” triggers in the L2 and R2 keys. Here again, it is above all a question of sensations, Sony affirming that the players will be able to “really feel the tension in [their] actions, like [when] bending a bow to shoot an arrow”. The controller can also offer different sensations depending on whether you use a machine gun or a shotgun, and thus reinforce the feeling of immersion

Also provided with a new CREATE button that Sony presents as an evolution of the SHARE button it replaces, the DualSense also intends to promote sharing between players. A set of microphones is also integrated there to converse online. Note of course to finish that the new controller from Sony also inaugurates a new design. The curves are more generous and there is a rather unusual marriage of colors with the consoler Nippon, who also claims to have tested this DualSense "with hands of different sizes" in order to satisfy a maximum of players. Comfort should also be at the rendezvous thanks to a reasonable weight, given the good autonomy announced at least, since it was not specified.
