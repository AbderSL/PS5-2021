Data sheet: AMD SoC, ray-tracing and SSD

Speculation is rife about the actual performance of the future Sony console. The architect of PlayStation 5, Mark Cerny, had nevertheless already  confirmed several elements in 2019 , before coming back to it with a little more details this year . Here are the main technical characteristics of the PS5


CPU

3rd generation AMD Ryzen
(Zen 2, 8 cores / 16 threads, 3.5 GHz, engraving in 7 nm)

GPU

AMD RDNA 2 customized
(36 computing units, 2.23 GHz, 10.3 TFLOPS)

RAM

16 GB (GDDR6)

Storage

825 GB custom SSD

Memory expansion

Yes, via M.2 SSD compatible internally, and USB externally

Optical drive

Yes, 4K HDR Blu-ray

Backward compatibility

Advertised for PS4 games at least

Other

3D audio
