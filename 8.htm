The Games

Sony has already confirmed that the PlayStation 5 will be backward compatible with PS4 and PlayStation VR games. The firm assured the Famitsu site that it was working on "full backward compatibility" with its current console, but it could go even further. There are rumors of backward compatibility with PS1, PS2 and PS3 games in addition to PlayStation 4 games. Information that has not yet been confirmed by Sony
