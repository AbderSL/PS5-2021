The design of the PlayStation 5

If until then we had to be satisfied with visuals inspired by patents and rumors, Sony confirmed in June 2020 the design of the PlayStation 5 and its Digital Edition. Curved lines, a black and white look, blue lighting reminiscent of PlayStation VR are therefore on the menu, as well as a support allowing the consoles to be placed vertically. The two PS5s will be accompanied by a set of suitable accessories. A headset, a multimedia remote control, a charging stand for two DualSense controllers (see details below) and a matching HD camera will also be marketed by Sony
