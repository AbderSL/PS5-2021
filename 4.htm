What release date for PlayStation 5?

The other big piece of information for October 2019 concerns the PS5 release date. Sony announces that its console “will be released in time for the holiday season 2020” , ie at the same time as Microsoft's Scarlett project , renamed Xbox Series X (see our dedicated file ). If the exact date has not been communicated, the firm wants to be on time for the 2020 holiday season and take advantage of this favorable period for sales.

Before its launch, the PlayStation 5 should be entitled to a presentation with great fanfare at an event organized by the manufacturer. There were rumors of an announcement before the end of 2019, but it is now known that Sony will wait until the end of 2020
